How the Pet Works
Stats
Happiness
Your pet's mood. Goes up when you feed, play, or pat it, and when you complete tasks & habits. Decays by −15 if you miss a whole day. Controls the mood expression (😍 → 😴).
Energy
How rested your pet is. Feeding and completing focus sessions restore energy. Playing costs −15 energy — your pet refuses to play when too tired (<10). Decays by −10 on missed days.
Progress
Reflects your real productivity today — resets each morning. Made up of: 40% todos done (up to 5), 40% habits completed, 20% focus timer sessions (up to 3).
